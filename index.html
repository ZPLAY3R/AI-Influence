🎯 What This Mod Does

AI Influence is a revolutionary total conversion mod that fundamentally transforms Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord by adding sophisticated artificial intelligence systems to manage virtually every aspect of the game world. It turns static NPCs into living, breathing characters with memory, personality, and agency while introducing dynamic world events, intelligent diplomacy, and complex relationship systems.

Core Concept: The mod replaces scripted interactions with real-time AI-generated content, making every playthrough unique and responsive to your actions. No two conversations, diplomatic events, or world developments are ever the same.
